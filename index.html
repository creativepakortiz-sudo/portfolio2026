About me

Creative is not what I do — it's how I think.

Born in Valencia in 1984, based in Madrid. I started in Fine Arts and ended up designing products used by millions — which is basically art at scale.

With over 15 years in the design field, I've worked across agencies, consultancies, startups, and large brands. I've led teams of six and shipped solo, both work great when the process is right.

I speak Scrum, I've done Kanban, and I always adapt the workflow to what the project actually needs — not the other way around.
